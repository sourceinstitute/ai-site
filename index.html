Burger Night

Burger Night is a place for tech founders to hang out. No talks, no investors, no business cards. Just us keeping it real and catching up.

Burger Night happens every month at a nice burger joint, usually a cheap one. (Burger Night is bootstrapper-friendly.)

If there is a Burger Night in your city, you have to ask around to find it. Some tech founders will know. Right now, there's a Burger Night in Rotterdam and Sofia. (It started in London, then, well, wtf London?)

If there's no Burger Night in your city, you can start one if you're a founder and you follow the rules below. You'll probably need to commit to it for 6 months to get it going. Then, you know, just let it exist. Please let us know when you start so we let everyone know it exists here. (Plus, we'll intro you to all the other Burger Night organisers in the world.)

Burger Night isn't advertised anywhere. It's not listed on any meetup site. It has no email list or LinkedIn group or Facebook page. It never has a sponsor. (We buy our own dinner, thank you.) Burger Night can't be owned or branded by a PR or events company. We keep it under the radar of all the hype, media, politics and other crap that gets in the way of running a tech business. It's self-organised, distributed, and obfuscated. It's our space.

We welcome every kind of person, we are open and inclusive to all. As long as they've taken the leap like us, as long as they've started a technology business. Let's keep it to founders only please.
